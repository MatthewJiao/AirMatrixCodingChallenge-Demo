In The Future

The drones are coming.

AIRMATRIX helps cities prepare for the emerging drone industry by building millimeter-precise highways in the sky.


Drones are becoming a major transport system. Within five years, there will be ten million drone flights around the world every day.
By 2020, enterprises and civil governments will have spent $13B on drones.

The drones are coming - and cities need to be ready. That’s where AIRMATRIX comes in.
AIRMATRIX builds millimeter-precise drone highways on a multi-layered grid system.
Helping high-density cities create a safe, scalable, and efficient transportation system.
Our software integrates directly with user autopilots.
We keep urban skies safe and under control while enabling scale.
